About Us

Bagiswori Secondary School is a community based academia founded by a group of socially motivated people in 2015 B.S. as a primary school. It is dedicated to produce qualified and competent human recources. With the length of time, BSS has extended its name and vision, and the academic sphere up to Master level.
Education is the formal act of imparting knowledge, developing the power of reasoning and judgement of academic maturity, Bagiswori Secondary School has best tried to materialize the above ideas with its glarious histroy and continuous sucess in the NEB results.It is the known as the most prominent and admired community-based academic institution throughout the action. It offer Science, Management, Arts-Humanities, Civil-Engineering-Diploma-Course, Computer Science, Business. More than 1500 students have made BSS their educational destination in class XI,XII,and altogether 3543 students have been pursuing their academic career in school,+2, TECS.
The immense enrolment of the students in this institution confirms the social credibility.BSS will leave no stone unturned to maintain this reliability and demonstrate solid performance.
